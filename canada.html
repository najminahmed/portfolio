Canada Highlights

For me, the most exciting part about Canada was being able to take the Niagara Falls Cruise and be right next to the water. It's crazy to see something so beautiful up close with a bunch of strangers you'll never see again.

We were also able to visit Skylon Tower which has an observation deck of the Falls in Canada and New York. That tower is insane since it also has a revolving restaurant, and an arcade and amusement area at the base. We were also able to go on the Niagara Skywheel, which is 175ft up in the air so it has nothing on the London Eye.
